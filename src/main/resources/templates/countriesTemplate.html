United States of America

The greatest country in the world. America has it all: forests, mountains, rolling hills, deserts, and yes even islands. Manifest destiny did not stop at the west coast. In 1959, Hawaii became the 50th State in the Union-2000 miles off the main land, deep into the Pacific Ocean.

Republic of Cabo Verde

Cape Verde is an archipelago in the central Atlantic Ocean, comprising ten volcanic islands. It is one of the most stable and developed countries in Africa. The culture here hatched from the intertwining of African and European roots.

Republic of Fiji

Fiji is an archipelago in the South Pacific Ocean consisting of more than 330 islands; only about a third of which are permanently inhabited. 87% of theh country's population live on the main two islands. Their economy is one of the most developed in the Pacific. Be prepared to get physcial in a rugby game to win over the locals.

Italian Republic

Italy: a birthplace of western civilization and culture. It is a peninsula in the middle of the Mediterranean Sea and is surrounded by several of its own islands. It is home to a great number of peoples and cultures.

Kingdom of Thailand

Thailand is a Southeast Asian nation with many islands that spot the Gulf of Thailand and Andaman Sea. Known for some of the world's best cuisine and effective martial arts.
